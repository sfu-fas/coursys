    Forming Groups

    If your instructor has asked you to form groups in CourSys, the procedure is:

    1. One group member should visit the course page and select: Manage Groups → Create New Group.
    2. Give the group a name and select the activities the group will be working on together (depending on your instructor's plan for the group).
    3. Invite the other members to join the group by giving their userid (whatever comes before @sfu.ca in their SFU email address).
    4. The other members will need to log in and accept the invitation. If group members don't accept the invitation, they cannot submit work and may not receive grades for it.

    Submitting as a Group

    Any group member can submit the work on behalf of the group. Of course, your group should coordinate as necessary.

    When another member of the group submits work on your behalf, you will receive a notification from CourSys.
