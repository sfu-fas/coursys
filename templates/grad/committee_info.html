    Supervisory Committee

    Potential Supervisor: An interim senior supervisor.

    Senior Supervisor: The primary or lead supervisor. The senior supervisor is also a member of the examining committee and provides financial support.

    Co-Supervisor: The co-supervisors jointly act as the senior supervisor. When a co-supervision arrangement is created, all documentation must be approved by both co-supervisors. The co-supervisors are also members of the examining committee and provide financial support.

    Supervisor: Member of both the supervisory and examining committee.

    Examining Committee

    Defence Chair: The chair of the student’s graduate program committee, or designate, who shall be a non-voting chair of the examining committee. If the chair of the graduate program committee is also on the student’s supervisory committee, the chair shall designate a member of faculty at this university, who is not a member of the student’s supervisory committee, as chair. Required for thesis master’s and doctoral programs.

    SFU Examiner: An examiner who is a member of faculty, or a person suitably qualified, who is not a member of the student's supervisory committee. Required for thesis master’s and doctoral programs.

    External Examiner: An external examiner who shall be specifically qualified in the field of the thesis and not be a member of faculty at the university. The external examiner should be a distinguished scholar with research and supervisory experience in the field of the thesis research. The examiner shall be free from potential conflict of interest. The external examiner may participate in-person, video/telephone conference or in absentia. The external examiner shall be asked to report on the thesis.

    Supervisory Committee: All members of the supervisory committee are also members of the examining committee.
